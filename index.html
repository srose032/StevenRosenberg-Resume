ER Physician

Growing up, the first language I was taught was Russian. I have always had a dream of becoming a doctor to be able to help people that can not help themselves. Throughout my life, my brother has made a big impact by helping me through school all the way. We have been born 10 years apart and both of us follow in the path of medicine.

Going through college was a rough time period for everybody. It is a place that we all learn in one way or another about what life has to offer. Here I have met many many people that I still keep in contact today. College is a great opportunity to gain much more connections to whatever field that you are applying to.

Aside from all the people and lessons I have learned through college, one important thing to remember is that this is your life that you can choose to do with it as you wish. College, at least for me, was a humbling transition, coming from a person that would be considered "too confident" or "too cocky" to a person who is "humble and respectful."
